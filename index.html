Routes:

/api/jokes/ get 10 jokes back

/api/jokes/:amount get as many jokes as you want by specifying the number

/api/jokes/:category /api/jokes/:category get 10 jokes by category (animal, "career", "celebrity", "dev", "explicit", "fashion", "food", "history", "money", "movie", "music", "political", "religion", "science", "sport", "travel")

/api/jokes/:category/:amount get as many jokes by category as you want
